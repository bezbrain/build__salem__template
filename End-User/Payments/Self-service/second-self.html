Apply for renewal The holder of a valid licence may apply for and obtain a renewal of the licence at any time from 30 days before the expiry date of the licence.Applicant apply and complete the form online, make payment, confirm payment at Board or Internal Revenue, proceed to VIO for test and visit FRSC Drivers Licence Centre for Biometric Capture.
