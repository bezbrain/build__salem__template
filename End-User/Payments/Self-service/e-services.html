Tax filling

Follow the steps below

  1. Download the Self-assessment form that applies to the tax you’re filing.
  2. Fill it appropriately.
  3. Then proceed to upload it alongside other required documents.
  4. Generate a reference number which you can use to check if your invoice has generated.
  5. If your invoice has been generated, you can go to the homepage and click on “Pay Now” to pay.

Taxes that need to be filed

S/N Description Frequency
1 Pay As You Earn Tax (PAYE) Monthly
2 Withholding Tax (WHT) Monthly
3 Company Income Tax (CIT) Annually
4 Education Tax Annually
5 Development levy One-off
